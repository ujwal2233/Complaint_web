Traffic Mitra receives 200+ complaints within 24 Hours, 76% resolved

Sumukh Kulkarni / Feb 23, 2025, 00:32 IST

Nagpur: The newly launched Traffic Mitra service by the city police received an overwhelming response from citizens, with 209 complaints registered across various categories within the first 24 hours. Of these, 153 issues were resolved, achieving an overall resolution rate of 76.5%.

According to the data shared by the police department, traffic related issues accounted for the highest number of complaints, with 88 registered and 62 resolved, marking a resolution rate of 70.4%. Complaints regarding challans and violations stood at 65, all of which were addressed promptly, reflecting a 100% resolution rate.

Parking issues drew 35 complaints, with 17 resolved, resulting in a 48.5% resolution rate. Encroachment issues were the least reported, with 21 complaints, of which only nine were resolved, giving a 42.8% resolution rate.

Speaking to TOI, DCP (traffic) Archit Chandak expressed satisfaction with the positive response from the public. Recounting an incident, he shared that a photograph highlighting an encroachment issue at Manewada Square was received through the Traffic Mitra platform. A police inspector was immediately dispatched, and the encroachment was removed.
